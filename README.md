_An art mod for cool delinquents_
---
Street Art adds the ability to coat any surface in paint

Use spray cans and paint balloons to add a splash of colour to the environment

![Spray paint covering a brick wall](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/n0uMUjJI/images/7687dedcf55fb308dbe3ce396b3b01fe0cd092b9.png)

![Paint balloons being thrown and leaving splashes on the floor](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/n0uMUjJI/images/fc5014853043aaeb445f308b7bf29d3135079175.gif)

---

Mob can also sometimes spawn with some sick new equipment, Rollerblades

Use them to slide around with custom movement, focused on maintaining and redirecting momentum

![Building up speed and coming to a stop](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/n0uMUjJI/images/d251ec4df0b5baa0a61f7611ec75bab891ec13da.gif)

![Walljumping?!](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/n0uMUjJI/images/2e114bf2493036094499082ea494ef3eb87df8ff.gif)

---

Optional compat with [Area Lib](https://modrinth.com/mod/area_lib) and [Slime Time](https://modrinth.com/mod/slime-time/versions)
